Everything your library needs

Nexus Launcher handles the heavy lifting so you can spend less time managing and more time playing.

📂

Folder-first scanning

Drop in any folder path — local drives, external disks, network shares — and Nexus will scan, detect, and catalogue every game it finds automatically.

🗃️

Smart organisation

Games are automatically grouped by genre, platform, and metadata. Custom tags and collections let you organise things your way on top of that.

⚡

One-click launch

No more hunting through File Explorer. Every game in your library is one click away — Nexus remembers the executable so you never have to.

🔍

Instant search

Find any title in milliseconds with full-text search across game names, genres, tags, and your own notes.

📊

Play-time tracking

Nexus quietly logs how long you've played each title, giving you a clear picture of what you're actually enjoying.

🎨

Rich metadata & art

Cover art, descriptions, and ratings are fetched automatically, turning a plain folder list into a visually rich library.
